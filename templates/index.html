Science in a Bag

Science in a Bag provides science kits for kids to learn and explore the world around them.

Our Mission

Science in a Bag is a company that creates science kits for kids to learn about the world around them. Our kits include everything needed for fun, educational experiments that can be done at home. Our goal is to inspire a love of science in children and make learning fun!

Learn More

About Us

We are the Unionville Chapter of Science in a Bag! We are a group of students who are passionate about science and want to share that passion with others. We believe that science should be accessible to everyone, and that learning should be fun!
